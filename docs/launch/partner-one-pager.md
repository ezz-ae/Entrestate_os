# Entrestate Partner One-Pager

## One-line value

Entrestate turns fragmented UAE real-estate signals into auditable, action-ready decision intelligence for teams, brokers, and partners.

## What partners get

- Structured market intelligence APIs and decision outputs
- Transparent evidence-backed scoring and recommendation logic
- Reports and artifacts ready for client, executive, and investor workflows
- Scalable content and documentation surfaces for distribution

## Product modules

- Decision Copilot (`/chat`, `/copilot`)
- Documentation intelligence stack (`/docs`, article graph)
- Reports library + generated report routes (`/reports/library`, `/reports/generated`)
- Market and scoring infrastructure (`/api/markets`, `/api/market-score/*`)

## Partner use cases

- Broker networks needing explainable opportunity ranking
- Portals/platforms embedding market insight widgets
- Advisory teams requiring evidence-linked investment memos
- Data partners distributing trusted market signals into downstream tools

## Integration model

- API-first consumption model
- Route-based outputs for report and artifact generation
- Tier-aware access model for partner and enterprise scaling
- Controlled evidence and confidence annotations in responses

## Why this is differentiated

- Intent-to-action architecture rather than simple search
- Evidence-stack hierarchy with explicit trust layers
- Deterministic fallback behavior for reliability
- Designed for operator decisions, not just dashboards
